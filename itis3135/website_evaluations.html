Website Evaluations

lingscars.com

This website is about leasing cars. This is a company in the UK that sells used cars. You can see who they are and what type of cars they have.

  • Contrast: First the website is very distracting.There is no good contrast on this website. The colors actually clash together.The colors are too bright as well
  • Repitiion:There is something different on each part of the page.
  • Alignment: This website is so distracting that I'm unsure about the alignment. But I don't believe everything looks aligned. There are words that goes over margins on the home page.
  • Proximity:Everything seems to be pushed together. It feels like they were trying to squeeze too much on each webpage and now it looks cluttered.

Ruckusmarketing.com

This website is about a digital agency that speciliaze in web design, app development, and branding. They show their work and the previous clients that they have worked with.

  • Contrast:I think the contrast is great. It's subtle and not over doing it. It's the right amount of white, black and orange.
  • Repition: Repition looks good. When I clicked through each page the menu never shifted. The header and footer remained the same on each page.
  • Alignment:Everything seems to be aligned well.Nothing seems out of place.
  • Proximity:The proximity looks good. There is nothing that is pushed together. It looks like they gave good margins.
