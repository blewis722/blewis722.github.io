Frequently Asked Questions

Where are you located? Do you travel?

I am based out of Charlotte, NC. Yes, I travel. This would be one of our first topics to discuss on your discovery call.

Do you help with posing?

Absolutely! This is a very common question. I like to pride myself on helping clients feel relaxed during our time together. This is generally one of the first compliments that I receive from couples.

How long does it take to receive our images from the shoot?

After your session, you will receive a proofing gallery within 24 to 48 hours. Once I have received your favorites, I will then edit and deliver within five to seven days. Wedding galleries and add-ons are delivered within four to six weeks after your wedding day

Do you provide videography?

Unfortunately, not at this time. However, I can provide references.

What if I need my engagement portraits back sooner?

Standard photo delivery is five to seven days. If you request to have your images much sooner, there will be a fee of $25 per image applied for each photo in your package.

Can I reschedule my photo session?

You are allowed one rescheduled appointment, but it must be rescheduled seven days before your scheduled shoot. Any shoot rescheduled within the seven days of your original date will require a reschedule fee of $75. Rescheduled shoots made within the appropriate timeframe will be scheduled on a new date with the original deposit.
